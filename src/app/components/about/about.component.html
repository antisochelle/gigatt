Candaba, classified as a first-class municipality in Pampanga rich in culture and heritage, has now adopted a tourist and funfair destination named The Great GIGATT Resort & Leisure Park.

The symbolic Ibon-Ebon Festival celebrating the scenic 32,000 hectare Candaba swamp, abundant in aquatic resources which also serves as a sanctuary for migratory birds, and the historical Pampanga River Cruise have inspired the developers of The Great GIGATT Resort to complement Candaba's richness in tradition.

Spanning 10 hectares of land, The Great GIGATT Resort is designed to cater to the whims of both the local and international market with its tourist-oriented amenities and traditional Filipino hospitality. We offer services and recreational facilities such as swimming pools and function halls, as well as attractions like the mini-zoo, boating area, and aviary and herbal garden that seek to bring enjoyment and relaxation to our guests.

Great for celebrations, family festivities, weddings, and even for business and corporate seminar meetings. Our facilities can manage and provide a comfortable and safe haven on your VIP stay.

The Great GIGATT Resort values every minute of your stay. Feel free to visit us and inform us of your wants so we can put smiles in your hearts.

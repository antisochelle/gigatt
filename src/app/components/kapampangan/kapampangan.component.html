The Kapampangan Language

A short list of useful and interesting Kapampangan words that might come in handy during your stay, or just for future reference.

SIMPLE PHRASES

"Mayap a abak"

Good morning

"Mayap a gatpanapun"

Good afternoon

"Mayap a bengi"

Good evening

"Malaus ko pu"

Welcome

"Dakal pung salamat"

Thank you

"Bay"

Goodbye

"Kaluguran daka"

I love you

"Pilan na kang banua?"

How old are you?

"Muli ku"

I will go home

"Nukarin ka (kanyan)?"

Where are you?

"Muli ku"

I will go home

"Sinali yang nasi"

He bought rice

"Makananu meng gewa ita?"

How did you do that?

"Kapilan ka mibalik?"

When will you be back?

"Atyu ku keni / Ati ku keni"

I am here
